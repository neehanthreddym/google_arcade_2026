Community Guidelines

Help us keep the community respectful, useful, and focused on learning.

  1. Be respectful and supportive.
    Treat all participants and facilitators with kindness. We are all here to learn.
  2. Do not spam links or promotional content.
    Keep the discussions focused on Google Cloud and Arcade activities.
  3. Do not share direct lab answers or violate learning integrity.
    Guide peers on how to solve problems, but do not provide direct cheat codes or completed files.
  4. Ask clear questions with screenshots and details.
    When asking for help, provide enough context so facilitators can unblock you efficiently.
  5. Help beginners patiently.
    Remember that everyone starts somewhere. Encourage those who are struggling.
  6. Use official sources for rules, deadlines, points, and rewards.
    Do not spread rumors about eligibility. Always refer to official Google communications.
  7. Keep discussions focused on Google Cloud learning.
    Off-topic discussions should be kept to a minimum to avoid cluttering support channels.
  8. Avoid sharing private participant information publicly.
    Protect your privacy and the privacy of others.
  9. Follow facilitator announcements and pinned messages.
    Read the pinned messages before asking common questions in the community groups.
