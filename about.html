About the Program

The Google Cloud Arcade Facilitator Program helps learners explore Google Cloud and AI technologies through hands-on games, skill badges, and monthly activities. By participating, you gain practical experience and can earn exciting rewards while building your cloud skills profile.

About This Community

This community was created to help participants stay consistent, understand monthly activities, complete skill badges, and build practical cloud skills with peer and facilitator support. We believe that learning cloud technology is easier and more fun when done together.
