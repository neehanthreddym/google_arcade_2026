About

Welcome to Google Cloud Arcade

Your gateway to gamified, no-cost learning on Google Skills.

Build technical skills in generative AI, prompt engineering, and diverse cloud technologies through engaging monthly games and hands-on labs.

Cloud Learning Platform

What is The Arcade?

Gamified Learning

Uses a game-like structure to make learning more engaging and accessible.

Monthly Games

New games are released each month, covering different aspects of Google Cloud technologies.

Hands-on Labs

Each game includes labs where learners can apply their knowledge and build practical skills.

No Cost

Participation in The Arcade is completely free of charge.

Skills You'll Master

  • Generative AI Games often focus on generative AI tools like Vertex AI and Generative AI Studio.
  • Prompt Engineering Learn how to fine-tune prompts to get the most out of Google Cloud's AI tools.
  • Various Cloud Technologies Cover topics like BigQuery, Firebase, Kubernetes, MongoDB, and more.

How The Arcade Works

Monthly Challenges: New games are available each month, offering a variety of challenges and labs.
Earning Points & Badges: Completing labs and games can earn you points and badges for swag and skill showcase.
Level Progression: Games often have different levels, allowing learners to progress at their own pace.
