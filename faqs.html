Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about the program and community.

1. What is Google Cloud Arcade?
It is a gamified learning platform where you can get hands-on experience with Google Cloud and AI technologies by completing labs and earning digital badges.
2. Who can join this community?
Anyone interested in learning Google Cloud, especially beginners and those enrolled in the Google Cloud Arcade Facilitator 2026 program.
3. Is prior cloud experience required?
No! The program is designed to be accessible for beginners with step-by-step guidance.
4. How do I create a Google Cloud Skills Boost account?
Visit the official Skills Boost website, sign in with your Google account, and follow the setup prompts. Check our Get Started page for details.
5. How do I make my profile public?
Go to your Skills Boost profile settings and toggle the "Make profile public" option. This is required for tracking your progress.
6. What are skill badges?
Skill badges are digital credentials awarded when you complete specific learning paths and challenge labs on Google Cloud Skills Boost.
7. How do I complete monthly Arcade activities?
Follow the schedule provided in our Monthly Roadmap and subscribe to the active games on the Arcade page.
8. How do I track my progress?
Your progress is automatically tracked on your public Skills Boost profile. We will also periodically request you to submit progress via a form.
9. What should I do if a lab is not working?
Check our Resources for troubleshooting guides, or ask for help in the community group using the recommended format.
10. Are rewards guaranteed?
Rewards depend on official Google Cloud rules and points earned. Always refer to official sources for eligibility.
11. Where can I check official rules and deadlines?
Always refer to the official Google Cloud Arcade website for the most accurate and up-to-date rules.
12. How do I contact the facilitators?
You can reach out via the community groups or use the form on our Contact page.
Disclaimer: This is a facilitator-led community website created to support learners participating in the Google Cloud Arcade Facilitator 2026 program. For official rules, dates, eligibility, rewards, and program updates, participants should always refer to official Google Cloud and Google Skills Arcade sources.
