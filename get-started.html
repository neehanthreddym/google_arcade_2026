Get Started

Follow this clear, step-by-step process to join the program and begin your Google Cloud learning journey.

Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Create or sign in to your Google Cloud Skills Boost account.
  2. Visit the official Google Cloud Arcade page.
  3. Subscribe or join the available Arcade activities.
  4. Make your Skills Boost profile public (required to track your progress).
  5. Join the WhatsApp or Telegram community.
  6. Follow the monthly roadmap on this website.
  7. Complete games, trivia, and skill badges.
  8. Share your progress when requested by facilitators.
  9. Ask for help whenever you get stuck!

Beginner Checklist

How to Ask for Help

When asking for help in the community groups or via the contact form, please share the following details so we can assist you faster:

  • Lab or game name: (e.g., GSP101 - Google Cloud Pub/Sub)
  • Step where you are stuck: (e.g., Task 3)
  • Screenshot of the error: (make sure it's clear)
  • What you already tried: (e.g., "I restarted the lab and re-entered the code")
  • Skills Boost profile link: (if needed)
