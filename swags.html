Official 2026 Prize Tiers

Prize eligibility is based on Arcade Points, limited tier capacity, and the official first-come, first-served waterfall system.

Arcade Trooper

50–74 Points

Prize capacity: First 6,000 eligible players

Arcade Ranger

75–94 Points

Prize capacity: First 4,000 eligible players

Arcade Champion

95–119 Points

Prize capacity: First 3,000 eligible players

Arcade Legend

120+ Points

Prize capacity: First 2,500 eligible players

Prize quantities are limited. If a higher tier reaches capacity, eligible participants may move to the next available lower tier through the official waterfall system. Earning the required points does not by itself guarantee a particular prize.

How the Waterfall System Works

1

Earn the required Arcade Points.

2

Eligible participants are considered for the highest tier they reached.

3

If that tier is full, eligibility may move to the next available lower tier.

The official Prize Counter is expected to open after the 2026 season ends. Dates, remaining capacity, eligibility rules, and fulfillment details may change.
