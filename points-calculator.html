Flow & Assumptions

  1. User enters public Google Skills profile URL and URL format is validated.
  2. Community service attempts to read publicly visible badges.
  3. Badges are filtered to Facilitator ’26 cohort dates (July 13 to September 14, 2026).
  4. Badges are classified by title heuristics and uncertain items are flagged for review.
  5. Base points are computed as: Arcade Game + floor(Skill Badges / 2).
  6. Milestone estimates use community thresholds: Trooper 50, Ranger 75, Champion 95, Legend 120.
